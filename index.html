Who We Are

Gorakhpur's Trusted Building Materials Partner

Aziz Traders was established with a single mission: to provide quality building materials at fair prices, with the honest, personalized service that only a local business can offer. Over the years, we have grown from a small hardware shop into one of Gorakhpur's most trusted destinations for plywood, doors, laminates, hardware fittings, paints, and interior building materials.

We believe that every construction project — whether a humble home or a large commercial complex — deserves premium materials. Our carefully curated product range reflects this belief, featuring only those brands and grades that meet our stringent quality standards.
